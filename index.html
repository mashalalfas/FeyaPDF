    02 · Open

    A file is a small intention.

    Feya opens PDFs the way they were meant to be opened — full-bleed, type-tuned, with margins that breathe. Pinch to zoom. Double-tap to fit width. Or just let the text settle into its line.

    • Full-bleed reading · 100% fidelity
    • Outline, bookmarks, layers preserved
    • Form fields, signatures, annotations
    • Offline · zero accounts · zero tracking

    03 · Mark

    Leave a trace.

    Highlight with the warmth of a pencil, not the coldness of a button. Six inks. Pressure-aware strokes. Every mark saved, every colour remembered. Or draw your own.

    ink:
